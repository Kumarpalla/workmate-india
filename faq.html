Frequently Asked Questions

Is WorkMate India free?

Yes. All calculators are completely free.

How is PF calculated?

Employee PF is generally 12% of Basic Salary.

How is ESI calculated?

Employee contribution is 0.75% and Employer contribution is 3.25%.

Is my salary data stored?

No. All calculations happen inside your browser.
